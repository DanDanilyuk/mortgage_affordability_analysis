Methodology

Cost-to-Income Multiplier: Represents how many years of income it would take to pay off a 30-year mortgage (including interest). Higher = lower affordability.

Data Sources

  • Earnings: BLS Series CES0500000011 (Average Weekly Earnings, Total Private)
  • Home Prices: FRED Series USAUCSFRCONDOSMSAMID (Zillow Home Value Index)
  • Mortgage Rates: FRED Series MORTGAGE30US (30-Year Fixed Rate Average)

Household Income: Calculated as 1.4x single income to account for dual-income households.
